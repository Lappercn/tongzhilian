### DuoYu Finance - AI Multi-Agent Investment Advisory System

Next-generation intelligent investment advisory system based on AI Agent technology, simulating the decision-making process of top fund companies.

- **Scenario**: Intelligent stock analysis, multi-perspective logical game theory, real-time market interpretation.
- **Highlights**:
    - **Multi-Agent Debate**: Multi-perspective game between bulls, bears, and moderators.
    - **Real-time Data Driven**: Access to real-time market data and news, rejecting hallucinations.
    - **3D Immersive Experience**: Virtual digital human broadcasting and visual reports.
